Motif · New

The greca border

A stepped-pyramid band drawn from Mesoamerican greca fretwork — gold steps over terracotta triangles on the indigo ground. It hugs the bottom edge of pool & check-in signs, replacing the older serrated trim.

{{ grecaBandTall }}
AnatomyGold step-frets (peaks) interlock with terracotta triangles (valleys); a peach key line sits between.
PlacementBottom edge only, full-bleed. Band height ≈ 6–9% of sign height. Never on body surfaces.
RestraintOne band per sign. It is the only decorative element allowed alongside the dimensional headline.
